
Demo title: 		Mouse

Demonstrates: 		Toon Shading

Short Description:	Shows cell-shading (a.k.a. Toon shading) effects applied on an animated model

Full Description:	
Cell-Shading is the "art" of rendering your objects to look like cartoons. This demo shows a dancing mouse with a hard black outline and banded shading to achieve the desired cartoon-look. Both effects are set-up using optimised software routines.

VGP: 			Supported
Polygon Count: 		3500
Vertex Count: 		2000
